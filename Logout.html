The platform may also automatically log you out after a period of inactivity for security purposes. You remain responsible for all activities conducted through your account prior to logout, and the platform shall not be held liable for interruptions, session expiration, or potential data loss resulting from logout actions, browser settings, or connectivity issues. The platform further reserves the right to update or modify its logout procedures and security measures at any time to maintain system integrity and user protection.
